Community & Awareness

TinkerHub’s Community of 15,000+ Members collectively aims to build strong tech knowledge among its members through peer learning and self-learning initiatives. We organize community meetups, learning stations, talks by industry leaders, career development programs, and many more.

Campus Communities

We have 70+ campus chapters across the state functioning in accordance with the Foundation's Vision & Mission. Promoting peer learning and self-learning, thereby upskilling the campus members is one of the key goals.

🎓 Learning & Tinkering

TinkerHub focuses on self-learning and peer learning methods over the conventional methods of learning. With learning activities throughout the year, making learners industry-ready. Activities such as bootcamps, hackathons, etc, focuses more on project building along with peer & self-learning.

Women In Tech

With the aim of bringing gender neutrality into the tech industries, TinkerHub organises women exclusive initiatives to empower and upskill the women members of the community.

💼 Career Opportunities

Our career development activities include CV/Resume building, interview readiness, LinkedIn Portfolio building, and many more.
